     Full Biography

   

Since I entered Miami Arts Studio 6-12, a magnet school, I started to learn about graphic design, That's when I realized that it's what I wanted to do, it's what I wanted to be. I knew from the start that I wanted to work as a graphic designer. I learned Photoshop and mastered many portions of it. I learned enough to earn some money by creating graphics for friends. Later in that same class, we were assigned an animation project. I've always watched movies and was fascinated by all the effects that made me wonder, "How is this even possible?". The project entailed creating a flying stork that would gently drop a child into the parents arms. That assignment was an eye opener for me. It hit me that this was what I was waiting for all along. Doing that animation made me see that this is my future or at least what I hope for. From that day on I would experiment with animations trying to make them look real or at least move correctly. It was hard at the start but like mostly everything in life it gets easier with time. So now currently I make animations using online guides or videos to further down the road be able to make my own ten minute film completely animated and voiced by me and some friends. Now all I can do is learn more and wait for the future to arrive.
