GOALS

Animator

My main goal right now is to be a professional animator for a large motion picture company. Being able to make an image move has been an astonishing experience and I hope it will be my future career.

Videogame Designer

Videogames are like interactive tri-dimensional animations that have a story. Whats more exciting than creating a story that you go through? Making videogames has always been something I've been drawn to.

Illustrator

To acomplish anything related to digital design, you need to create the base of what you will animate, edit or use for any project. This base is illustration; the ability to create an image. Anyone can grab an image off the internet but being able to create the image is special.

Astrophysicist

Science has always been my favorite subject, especially astrophysics. If I don't enter a digital design career, I plan to work in laboratories and deal with important topics. Astrophysics deals with space, a topic I've always been curious about and interested in. 

     Full Biography

   

The one thing that got me to develop an interest in digital design has been movies and cartoons. Since I was eight I would be astonished by how real movies looked even though most of it was fake. I grew a large interest in in it and decided to actually learn it and possibly be good at it. Like most things it was bad at the start, trying to make things but they never really looked good. After finishing elementary school, I applied to a digital design magnet school, Miami Arts Studio 6-12. That’s what I really learned how to create masterpieces using Photoshop, illustrator even animate. In that class I learned things I never thought I could accomplish. All the projects I’ve done in the class have each helped me in improving my digital design skills. At that point I realized that this could be my profession in the future, creating practically anything from nothing is something that not many can do. I don’t exactly plan on being a digital designer, but an animator which still requires digital design skills. Going back to why I joined the school, I’ve always wanted to create mayor motion pictures, and now I can. I have learned animation and I keep trying to master animation for that is what I’m aiming for. Animation plus digital design in my opinion is a combination that I’m proud of having.
